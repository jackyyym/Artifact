Built project is included under /Artifact/Builds/Build V1.0/, simply run using SteamVR and a Vive headset.
To build project, use unity 2018.

Controls:
   Left Touchpad:  horizontal movement
   Right Touchpad: click left and right sides of touchpad to turn left and right
   Trigger:        interact
   ESC:            Exit
